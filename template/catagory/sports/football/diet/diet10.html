    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,400 to 2,800 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 scrambled eggs
    1 slice of whole-grain toast with avocado
    1 piece of fruit (apple or banana)
    1 glass of low-fat milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A small smoothie with banana and spinach

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (150g) or turkey breast
    1.5 cups of brown rice or quinoa
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    A protein bar or Greek yogurt

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (fish or chicken, 150-200g)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (asparagus, green beans)
    1 medium sweet potato or whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 2-3 liters of water throughout the day.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
