    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,500 to 2,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 scrambled eggs or paneer bhurji
    2 slices of whole wheat toast with peanut butter
    1 bowl of oatmeal with fruits and nuts
    1 glass of full-fat milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Greek yogurt with honey
    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 banana or smoothie

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or paneer
    Brown rice or chapati
    Steamed vegetables with olive oil
    1 bowl of chickpeas or dal
    1 glass of fruit juice (orange or mixed)

    Afternoon Snack:

    Peanut butter sandwich
    1 pear
    1 glass of milk

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or chicken
    Sweet potatoes or chapati
    Mixed salad (spinach, tomatoes)
    1 bowl of lentils
    1 glass of buttermilk

    Before Bed:

    Warm milk with honey
    Dried fruits or raisins

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-8 glasses of water throughout the day.
    Include electrolyte-rich drinks like coconut water after training.

    Before Training:

    A small snack 1-2 hours before: A banana with peanut butter or multigrain crackers.

    After Training:

    A post-workout meal: Protein shake with a piece of fruit (banana or apple) within 30-60 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks and drinks (candy, soda)
    Fried foods (french fries, fried chicken)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
    Excessive desserts (cakes, cookies)
