    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,800 to 2,300 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Whole grain toast with peanut butter
    4 scrambled eggs (boiled or omelet)
    A piece of fruit (banana or berries)
    1 glass of full-fat milk or protein shake

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts)
    Greek yogurt with honey and granola
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or tofu
    Brown rice or quinoa
    A serving of vegetables (steamed or stir-fried)
    1 bowl of chickpeas
    A glass of fruit juice

    Afternoon Snack:

    A piece of fruit (apple or pear)
    Multigrain crackers with hummus
    A handful of seeds (pumpkin or sunflower)

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish (salmon or tuna)
    Sweet potatoes
    A serving of vegetables (steamed or stir-fried)
    A small salad with olive oil dressing
    1 bowl of dal
    1 glass of buttermilk

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-8 glasses of water throughout the day.
    Include electrolyte-rich drinks like coconut water after training.

    BEFORE TRAINING:

    A small snack 1-2 hours before: A banana or a protein bar.

    AFTER TRAINING:

    A post-workout meal: Protein shake with a small piece of fruit (apple or pear) within 30-60 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks and drinks (candy, soda)
    Fried and fast food (french fries, burgers)
    Processed foods (instant noodles, chips)
