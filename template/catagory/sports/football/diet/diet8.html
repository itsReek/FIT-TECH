    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,400–2,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 scrambled eggs or paneer bhurji
    2 slices of whole wheat toast with peanut butter
    1 bowl of oatmeal with fruits and nuts
    1 glass of full-fat milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Greek yogurt with honey
    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 banana or smoothie

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or paneer
    Brown rice or chapati
    Steamed veggies with olive oil
    1 bowl of chickpeas or dal
    1 glass of fruit juice (orange or mixed)

    Afternoon Snack:

    Peanut butter sandwich
    1 pear
    1 glass of milk

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or chicken
    Sweet potatoes or chapati
    Mixed salad (spinach, tomatoes)
    1 bowl of lentils
    1 glass of buttermilk

    BEFORE BED:

    Warm milk with honey
    Dried fruits or raisins

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 2.5–3 liters of water per day.
    Include coconut water after training for electrolytes.

    BEFORE TRAINING:

    A fruit smoothie with yogurt or a handful of mixed nuts.

    AFTER TRAINING:

    A protein shake with fruit or toast within 30 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks and beverages
    Processed and fried foods
    Packaged fast food
