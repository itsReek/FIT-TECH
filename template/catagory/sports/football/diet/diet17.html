    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,800 to 3,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 eggs (boiled or scrambled)
    1 slice of whole-grain toast with peanut butter
    1 medium-sized fruit (banana or orange)
    1 glass of low-fat milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A protein shake or bar

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast (200g) or lean beef
    2 cups of brown rice or whole-grain pasta
    Mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)

    Afternoon Snack:

    Hummus with carrot sticks or whole-grain crackers

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (fish or chicken, 200g)
    1 medium baked potato or sweet potato
    Steamed broccoli or spinach

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 2-3 liters of water throughout the day.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
