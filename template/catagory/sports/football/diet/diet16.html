    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,600 to 3,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 eggs (boiled or scrambled)
    2 slices of whole-grain toast with avocado
    1 banana or apple
    1 glass of low-fat milk or a smoothie

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds or walnuts

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast (150-200g) or turkey
    1.5 cups of quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots)

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt with mixed berries and honey

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (fish or chicken, 200g)
    1 medium baked sweet potato or a serving of whole-grain pasta
    Mixed greens salad with olive oil and vinegar

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 2-3 liters of water throughout the day.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
