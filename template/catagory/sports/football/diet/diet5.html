FitTech

    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,000–2,300 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 scrambled eggs or paneer
    1 slice of whole wheat toast with peanut butter
    1 bowl of oatmeal with fruit and nuts
    1 glass of milk or protein shake

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Greek yogurt with granola
    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast
    Brown rice
    Steamed vegetables
    1 bowl of dal
    1 glass of fresh fruit juice

    Afternoon Snack:

    Peanut butter sandwich
    1 apple
    1 glass of milk

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish
    Sweet potatoes
    Mixed salad with olive oil
    1 bowl of lentils
    1 glass of buttermilk

    BEFORE BED:

    Warm milk with honey
    Dates

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 2–2.5 liters of water throughout the day.
    Include coconut water or an electrolyte drink after intense exercise.

    BEFORE TRAINING:

    A snack 1-2 hours before: A banana with peanut butter or whole wheat toast with avocado.

    AFTER TRAINING:

    A post-workout meal: Protein shake with fruits or yogurt within 30-60 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary cereals and soft drinks
    Fast food and fried snacks
    Processed foods with low nutritional value
