    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,000 to 2,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    6 scrambled eggs or an omelet
    2 slices of whole grain toast
    1 bowl of oatmeal with fruits and nuts
    1 glass of milk or protein shake

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Greek yogurt with honey
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or fish
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed vegetables
    1 bowl of lentils or chickpeas

    Afternoon Snack:

    Hummus with veggie sticks
    A protein bar or shake

    Dinner:

    Lean meat (chicken, turkey) or tofu
    Sweet potatoes or whole wheat pasta
    Mixed salad with olive oil
    1 bowl of dal

    Before Bed:

    Cottage cheese or Greek yogurt
    A handful of nuts or seeds

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 8-10 glasses of water throughout the day.
    Include electrolyte-rich drinks after training.

    Before Training:

    A small snack 1-2 hours before: A banana with peanut butter or granola.

    After Training:

    A post-workout meal: Protein shake with a piece of fruit within 30-60 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks and drinks (candy, soda)
    Fried foods (potato chips, french fries)
    Processed foods (instant noodles, chips)
    Excessive desserts (cakes, cookies)
