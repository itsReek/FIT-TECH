FitTech

    • FOOTBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,300–2,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 scrambled eggs
    2 slices of whole wheat toast with avocado
    1 bowl of oatmeal with nuts (almonds, walnuts) and fruit
    1 glass of full-fat milk or protein shake

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Greek yogurt with honey
    A handful of mixed nuts and seeds
    1 banana or smoothie

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast
    Brown rice or quinoa
    Steamed veggies (zucchini, carrots)
    1 bowl of dal or chickpeas
    1 glass of pomegranate juice

    Afternoon Snack:

    Peanut butter sandwich
    1 apple
    1 glass of milk

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or tofu
    Sweet potatoes
    Mixed salad with olive oil
    1 bowl of lentils
    1 glass of lassi or buttermilk

    BEFORE BED:

    Warm milk with honey
    Dates or dried fruits

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 2.5 liters of water per day.
    Add an electrolyte drink or coconut water after intense activity.

    BEFORE TRAINING:

    A banana with peanut butter or a handful of nuts.

    AFTER TRAINING:

    Protein shake or yogurt with fruit within 30–60 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary energy drinks and sodas
    Deep-fried snacks
    Highly processed or packaged foods
