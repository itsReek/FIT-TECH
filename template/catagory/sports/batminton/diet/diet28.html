FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,700 - 3,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 boiled eggs with avocado toast (whole grain)
    1 bowl of oats with almond milk and fresh fruit
    1 glass of fresh juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds or cashews
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or paneer (200g)
    Brown rice or quinoa
    Stir-fried vegetables (broccoli, carrots, spinach)

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 banana
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or tofu (200g)
    1 whole wheat chapati or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk or a smoothie

    HYDRATION:

    3.5 to 4 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or sports drinks after training sessions

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Fried foods
    Processed junk food
