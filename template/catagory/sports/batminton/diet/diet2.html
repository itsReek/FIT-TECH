FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,300 - 1,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 slice of whole wheat toast with peanut butter
    1 boiled egg
    1 small banana

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Handful of walnuts
    1 small yogurt

    Lunch:

    Small serving of grilled chicken or chickpeas
    1 small serving of quinoa or brown rice
    Mixed steamed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 pear
    1 glass of milk

    Dinner:

    Grilled chicken or fish (75g)
    1 small whole wheat chapati
    Salad with olive oil and lemon dressing

    BEFORE BED:

    1 cup of warm milk or herbal tea

    HYDRATION:

    Drink at least 2 liters of water throughout the day
    Hydrate with sports drinks or coconut water after games.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Deep-fried snacks
    Sugary desserts
    Processed junk foods
