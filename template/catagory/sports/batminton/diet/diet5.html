    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,500 - 1,700 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Small bowl of oats with berries and honey
    1 boiled egg
    1 glass of orange juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 small handful of almonds
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (75g)
    1 small portion of brown rice
    Steamed green beans and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    1 small yogurt (unsweetened)

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (75g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Salad with cucumbers and tomatoes

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    At least 2 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or sports drinks during/after matches.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary desserts
    Processed junk food
    Deep-fried snacks
