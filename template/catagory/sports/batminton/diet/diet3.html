FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,400 - 1,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 small bowl of porridge with fruit and nuts
    1 boiled egg
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 small apple
    1 handful of mixed nuts

    Lunch:

    Grilled tofu or lean chicken
    1 small portion of whole grain bread or quinoa
    Green salad

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt with honey
    1 banana

    Dinner:

    Grilled salmon or chicken (75g)
    1 small whole wheat chapati
    Stir-fried vegetables

    BEFORE BED:

    1 cup of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 2 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or electrolyte drinks during games.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Fried foods
    Processed snacks
    Sugary sodas
