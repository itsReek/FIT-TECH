FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,500 - 3,700 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 boiled eggs with avocado toast (whole grain)
    1 bowl of oats with berries and chia seeds
    1 glass of fresh juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 cup of yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or paneer (200g)
    Brown rice or quinoa
    Steamed spinach and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 banana
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or tofu (200g)
    1 whole wheat chapati or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil and lemon

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk or smoothie

    HYDRATION:

    3.5 to 4 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or electrolyte drinks during/after intense training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed foods
    Fried snacks
