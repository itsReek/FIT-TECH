    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,300 - 2,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Small bowl of quinoa with nuts and berries
    1 omelet with vegetables
    1 glass of fresh juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or fish (150g)
    1 serving of whole grain pasta
    Mixed salad with olive oil dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small apple
    A handful of yogurt-covered raisins

    Dinner:

    Grilled chicken or fish (150g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Steamed vegetables (carrots, beans)

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    3 liters of water daily
    Drink coconut water or herbal teas.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Junk food
    Soft drinks
    Processed snacks
