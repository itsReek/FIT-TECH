FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,300 - 2,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Oatmeal with sliced bananas and almonds
    2 boiled eggs
    1 glass of fresh orange juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or fish (150g)
    1 serving of brown rice
    Steamed broccoli and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small apple
    A handful of mixed seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (150g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Mixed vegetable salad

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    3 liters of water daily
    Herbal teas or coconut water during/after matches.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Fast food
    Sugary drinks
    Processed foods
