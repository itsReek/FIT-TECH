FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,700 - 1,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Small bowl of oatmeal with mixed fruits and nuts
    1 boiled egg
    1 glass of fresh juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 small apple
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Lunch:

    Grilled tofu or lean chicken
    1 small serving of quinoa
    Steamed broccoli and spinach

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt with honey
    1 banana

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (75g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Stir-fried vegetables

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2 to 2.5 liters of water daily
    Drink coconut water or electrolyte drinks during/after matches.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Fried foods
    Sugary drinks
    Processed snacks
