FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,500 - 2,700 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 bowl of whole grain cereal with milk
    2 boiled eggs
    1 glass of fresh juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or paneer (150g)
    1 serving of brown rice
    Mixed salad with olive oil

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small banana
    A handful of mixed seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or chicken (150g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots)

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk or a protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or electrolyte drinks during/after training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary foods
    Fried snacks
    Highly processed snacks
