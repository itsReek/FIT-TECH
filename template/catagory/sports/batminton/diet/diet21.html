FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,000 - 3,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 boiled eggs with avocado toast (whole grain)
    1 bowl of oatmeal with almonds and berries
    1 glass of fresh orange juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 cup of yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or tofu (200g)
    Brown rice or quinoa
    Steamed broccoli and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 banana
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled salmon or paneer (200g)
    1 whole wheat chapati or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk or protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    3 to 3.5 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or electrolyte drinks after intense training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Fried foods
    Processed junk food
