FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,200 - 1,400 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Small bowl of oatmeal with milk
    1 boiled egg
    1 glass of orange juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 banana
    Handful of almonds

    Lunch:

    Small portion of grilled chicken or tofu
    1 small serving of brown rice
    Steamed broccoli and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    1 yogurt (unsweetened)

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish (75g) or paneer
    1 small whole wheat chapati or small portion of quinoa
    Cucumber and tomato salad

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk with turmeric

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 1.5 to 2 liters of water throughout the day
    Add coconut water or electrolyte-rich drinks after practice.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Fried snacks (chips, pakoras)
    Sugary sodas
    Candies and processed sugar treats
    Excessive salt or processed foods
