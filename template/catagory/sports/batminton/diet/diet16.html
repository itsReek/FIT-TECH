    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,500 - 2,700 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Small bowl of oats with berries, chia seeds, and almond milk
    2 boiled eggs
    1 glass of fresh juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds or walnuts
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (150g)
    1 small serving of brown rice or whole grain bread
    Steamed vegetables (spinach, broccoli)

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small apple or pear
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or chicken (150g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Mixed salad with olive oil and lemon

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or sports drinks during/after matches

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed junk foods
    Fried foods
