FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,700 - 2,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Whole wheat toast with avocado and poached eggs (2 eggs)
    A bowl of oatmeal with mixed berries and chia seeds
    1 glass of fresh juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds or cashews
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or paneer (150g)
    1 portion of brown rice
    Stir-fried vegetables (broccoli, spinach, carrots)

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or chicken (150g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Mixed salad with olive oil and vinegar

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk or a protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or electrolyte drinks during/after training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary foods
    Fast food
    Highly processed snacks
