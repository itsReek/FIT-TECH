    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,400 - 3,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3-4 eggs scrambled with vegetables
    1 bowl of oatmeal with honey and nuts
    1 glass of fresh juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    1 cup of yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or fish (250g)
    1 portion of brown rice or quinoa
    Steamed broccoli and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled tofu or paneer (250g)
    1 whole wheat chapati or brown rice
    Mixed salad with olive oil

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk or a protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    4 to 4.5 liters of water daily
    Coconut water after workouts

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Fried foods
    Processed junk food
