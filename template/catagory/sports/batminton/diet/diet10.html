FitTech

    • BADMINTON DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,000 - 2,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Whole wheat toast with peanut butter
    1 boiled egg
    1 banana

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or chickpeas
    1 portion of quinoa or whole grain rice
    Steamed vegetables (carrots, broccoli)

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 orange or pear
    A handful of nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (100g)
    1 small portion of couscous or whole wheat bread
    Mixed salad with olive oil and lemon

    BEFORE BED:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drinks after intense training sessions.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary sodas
    Deep-fried snacks
    Processed foods
