FitTech

    • BASEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,800 - 1,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 boiled egg
    1 slice of whole grain toast
    1 small bowl of mixed fruits

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of nuts
    1 orange

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or fish (120g)
    Whole wheat pasta with olive oil
    Mixed salad with lemon dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 protein bar
    A handful of berries

    Dinner:

    Baked tofu or fish (120g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Stir-fried vegetables

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 to 2 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drink during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Fast food
    Sugary drinks
