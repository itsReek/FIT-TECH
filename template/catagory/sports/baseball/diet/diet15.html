FitTech

    • BASEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,200 - 2,300 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 boiled eggs
    1 slice of whole grain toast
    1 small bowl of mixed fruits

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of cashews
    1 apple

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (200g)
    Brown rice
    Steamed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 protein shake
    1 banana

    Dinner:

    Baked fish (200g)
    Quinoa
    Mixed salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drink during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed food
    Fast food
