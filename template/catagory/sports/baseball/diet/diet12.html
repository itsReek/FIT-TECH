FitTech

    • BASEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,100 - 2,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 scrambled eggs
    2 slices of whole grain bread
    1 small bowl of yogurt with fruits

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 orange

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or turkey (200g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad with vinaigrette

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 protein bar
    A handful of berries

    Dinner:

    Baked salmon or tofu (200g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Stir-fried vegetables

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drink during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Processed snacks
    Sugary drinks
    Fast food
