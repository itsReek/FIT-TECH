FitTech

    • BASEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,900 - 2,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 eggs (scrambled or boiled)
    1 slice of whole grain bread
    1 small bowl of yogurt with fruits

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 pear

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or turkey (150g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad with vinaigrette

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 protein shake
    1 banana

    Dinner:

    Baked salmon or tofu (150g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Steamed vegetables

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2 to 2.5 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drink during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary drinks
    Processed snacks
    Fast food
