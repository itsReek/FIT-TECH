FitTech

    • BASEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,900 - 2,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 boiled eggs
    1 whole grain bagel with cream cheese
    1 glass of orange juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of trail mix
    1 apple

    Lunch:

    Grilled fish or chicken (150g)
    Couscous or brown rice
    Steamed broccoli

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small smoothie
    A handful of almonds

    Dinner:

    Stir-fried tofu or chicken (150g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Mixed salad with dressing

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2 to 2.5 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drink during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed food
    Fast food
