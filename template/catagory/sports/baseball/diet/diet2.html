FitTech

    • BASEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,600 to 1,700 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 boiled egg with whole grain toast
    1 small bowl of muesli with yogurt
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled turkey or tofu (100g)
    Brown rice or whole wheat pasta
    Steamed peas and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    A handful of mixed seeds

    Dinner:

    Baked chicken or paneer (100g)
    1 multigrain chapati
    Mixed vegetable salad with lemon dressing

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of almond milk

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 to 2 liters of water daily
    Coconut water during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sweetened cereals
    French fries
    Packaged snacks
