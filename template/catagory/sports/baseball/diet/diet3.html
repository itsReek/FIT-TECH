FitTech

    • BASEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,700 to 1,800 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 boiled eggs with whole wheat toast
    1 small bowl of chia pudding with fresh fruit
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of pistachios
    1 pear

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (100g)
    Couscous or whole wheat bread
    Steamed zucchini and bell peppers

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 banana
    A handful of mixed seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (100g)
    1 whole wheat roti
    Mixed vegetable stir-fry

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 to 2 liters of water daily
    Sports drink during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary pastries
    Fried snacks
    Instant noodles
