    • TENNIS DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,600 - 1,800

    Breakfast:

    1 cup of oatmeal
    1 boiled egg
    1 orange

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 small handful of walnuts
    1 glass of fruit juice

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (100g)
    Brown rice
    Steamed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 granola bar
    1 pear

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish (150g)
    Whole wheat pasta
    Mixed salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of low-fat milk

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Processed snacks
    Sugary drinks
    High-fat dairy
