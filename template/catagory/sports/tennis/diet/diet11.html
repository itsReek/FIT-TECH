    • TENNIS DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,200 - 2,400

    Breakfast:

    1 serving of oatmeal
    1 tablespoon of almond butter
    1 banana

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 apple
    A handful of cashews

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast (150g)
    Brown rice
    Steamed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 protein shake
    1 small fruit

    Dinner:

    Lean meat or fish (150g)
    Salad with olive oil
    Quinoa or whole grain

    Before Bed:

    1 cup of low-fat yogurt

    HYDRATION:

    2 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Chips and cookies
    Sugary drinks
    Fried foods
