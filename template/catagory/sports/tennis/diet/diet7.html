    • TENNIS DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,800 - 2,000

    Breakfast:

    1 cup of cereal with milk
    1 fruit
    1 small protein shake

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 glass of water

    Lunch:

    Grilled salmon or chicken (150g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed broccoli

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small protein bar
    1 orange

    Dinner:

    Lean beef or fish (150g)
    Mixed vegetable stir-fry
    1 whole wheat chapati

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Fast food
    Soda
