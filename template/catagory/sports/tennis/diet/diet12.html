    • TENNIS DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,300 - 2,500

    Breakfast:

    3 egg omelet with vegetables
    1 slice of whole-grain bread
    1 cup of mixed fruit

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 pear
    A handful of walnuts

    Lunch:

    Grilled fish (150g)
    Couscous or brown rice
    Steamed broccoli or spinach

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 protein smoothie
    1 piece of dark chocolate

    Dinner:

    Roasted chicken (150g)
    Salad with avocado
    1 whole grain roll

    Before Bed:

    1 cup of herbal tea

    HYDRATION:

    2-2.5 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-sugar cereals
    Fried snacks
    Energy drinks
