    • TENNIS DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,000 - 2,200

    Breakfast:

    2 scrambled eggs
    1 slice of whole-grain toast
    1 banana

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 small apple
    1 tablespoon of peanut butter

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or turkey (150g)
    Mixed salad with olive oil
    Quinoa or brown rice

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 protein shake
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Fish or lean meat (150g)
    Steamed vegetables
    1 whole wheat chapati

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-sugar snacks
    Processed foods
    Sugary drinks
