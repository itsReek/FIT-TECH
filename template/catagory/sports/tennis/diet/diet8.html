    • TENNIS DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,900 - 2,100

    Breakfast:

    1 cup of oatmeal
    1 fruit smoothie
    1 boiled egg

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 small handful of almonds
    1 glass of water

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (150g)
    Brown rice or whole grain pasta
    Steamed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small yogurt
    1 piece of fruit

    Dinner:

    Fish or lean meat (150g)
    Mixed vegetable stir-fry
    1 whole wheat chapati

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Fast food
    Soda
