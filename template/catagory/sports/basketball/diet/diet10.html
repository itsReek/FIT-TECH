    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,100 to 2,300 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 boiled eggs or an omelet
    1 bowl of oatmeal with honey and fruits
    1 glass of low-fat milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    A small fruit (banana or pear)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (150g) or fish
    1 cup of whole wheat pasta or brown rice
    Mixed salad with dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt or a protein bar
