    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,200 to 2,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 eggs (boiled or scrambled)
    1 bowl of whole-grain cereal with milk
    1 banana

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    A small fruit (apple or orange)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (200g) or fish
    1 cup of brown rice or quinoa
    Mixed vegetable salad

    Afternoon Snack:

    A protein shake or Greek yogurt

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (chicken or fish)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (broccoli, spinach)
    1 small whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 5-6 glasses of water throughout the day

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
