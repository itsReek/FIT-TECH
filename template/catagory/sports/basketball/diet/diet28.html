    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,700 to 4,300 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    5 eggs (scrambled or boiled)
    1 bowl of whole-grain cereal topped with nuts and fruits
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    A small fruit (banana or peach)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (400g) or tofu
    2 cups of whole wheat pasta
    Mixed salad with dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    A protein bar or smoothie

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (chicken or fish)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (spinach, green beans)
    1 small whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-7 glasses of water throughout the day.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
