    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,300 to 2,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 egg whites with 1 whole egg
    1 bowl of oatmeal topped with berries
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    A piece of fruit (apple or pear)

    Lunch:

    Grilled turkey or chicken (200g)
    1 cup of quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed vegetables (carrots, broccoli)

    Afternoon Snack:

    A protein bar or cottage cheese

    Dinner:

    Lean beef or fish (200g)
    1 serving of whole wheat pasta
    Mixed green salad

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 5-6 glasses of water throughout the day

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
