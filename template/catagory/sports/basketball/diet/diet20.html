    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,800 to 3,300 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 eggs (boiled or scrambled)
    1 bowl of oatmeal with nuts
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    A small fruit (orange or pear)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (300g) or fish
    1 cup of brown rice
    Mixed salad with dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt or a protein shake

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (chicken or fish)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (carrots, peas)
    1 small whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 7-8 glasses of water throughout the day

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
