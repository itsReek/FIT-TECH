    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,300 to 3,800 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 eggs (boiled or scrambled)
    1 bowl of oatmeal topped with berries and nuts
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    A small fruit (apple or kiwi)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (400g) or turkey
    2 cups of quinoa or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad with dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt or a protein shake

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (fish or chicken)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots)
    1 small whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-7 glasses of water throughout the day.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
