    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,600 to 1,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 boiled eggs or an omelet
    1 bowl of oatmeal with fruits and nuts
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    A small fruit (banana or apple)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (150g) or fish
    1 cup of whole wheat pasta or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad with dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt or a smoothie

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (chicken or fish)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (broccoli, spinach)
    1 small whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 5-6 glasses of water throughout the day

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
