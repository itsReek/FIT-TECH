    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,300 to 2,700 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 egg whites with 2 whole eggs
    1 bowl of whole grain cereal with milk
    1 glass of fresh fruit juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    A small fruit (kiwi or orange)

    Lunch:

    Grilled salmon or chicken (250g)
    1 cup of brown rice or whole grain bread
    Steamed broccoli and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    A smoothie with protein powder and fruits

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (chicken, turkey, or fish)
    1 serving of whole grain pasta
    Mixed salad with olive oil dressing

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 5-6 glasses of water throughout the day

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
