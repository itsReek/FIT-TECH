    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,500 to 4,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 eggs (scrambled or boiled)
    1 bowl of whole-grain cereal with milk
    1 banana

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    A small fruit (kiwi or orange)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (350g) or turkey
    2 cups of whole wheat pasta
    Mixed salad with olive oil dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    A protein bar or smoothie

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (chicken or fish)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (spinach, peas)
    1 small whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-7 glasses of water throughout the day.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
