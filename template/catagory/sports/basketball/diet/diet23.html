    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,000 to 3,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 eggs (boiled or scrambled)
    1 bowl of oatmeal with berries and honey
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    A small fruit (apple or orange)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (300g) or turkey
    1.5 cups of brown rice or quinoa
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt or a protein shake

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (fish or chicken)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots)
    1 small whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-7 glasses of water throughout the day.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
