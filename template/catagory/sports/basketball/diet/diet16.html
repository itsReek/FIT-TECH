    • BASKEBALL DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,400 to 2,800 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 scrambled eggs
    1 bowl of oatmeal topped with fruits and honey
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    A small fruit (orange or banana)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (200g) or tofu
    1 cup of whole wheat pasta
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    A protein bar or smoothie

    Dinner:

    Lean protein (fish or chicken)
    1 serving of steamed vegetables (spinach, peas)
    1 small whole-grain roll

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 5-6 glasses of water throughout the day

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
