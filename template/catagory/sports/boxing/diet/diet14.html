    • BOXING DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,300 - 2,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 egg whites and 1 whole egg
    1 slice of whole grain toast
    1 small apple

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 protein shake
    A handful of almonds

    Lunch:

    Tuna salad with mixed greens
    Whole grain crackers

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small bowl of Greek yogurt with honey

    Dinner:

    Chicken breast (200g)
    Brown rice
    Steamed carrots

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-sugar snacks
    Processed foods
    Sugary drinks
