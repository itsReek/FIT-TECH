    • BOXING DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,800 - 3,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    Smoothie with spinach, banana, and protein powder
    1 slice of whole grain toast with avocado

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Handful of trail mix
    1 orange

    Lunch:

    Grilled salmon
    Quinoa salad with veggies

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 cup of Greek yogurt
    A few slices of pineapple

    Dinner:

    Chicken stir-fry with broccoli and bell peppers
    Brown rice

    1 glass of milk or protein shake

    3 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-calorie junk foods
    Sugary drinks
    High-sodium snacks
