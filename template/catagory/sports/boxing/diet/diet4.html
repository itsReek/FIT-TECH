FitTech

    • BOXING DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,800 - 1,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 poached eggs
    1 slice of multigrain toast
    1 small bowl of oatmeal with nuts and fruits
    1 glass of orange juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of hazelnuts
    1 kiwi

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or fish (150g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed asparagus and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    1 small cup of cottage cheese

    Dinner:

    Baked fish or tofu (150g)
    1 whole wheat pita
    Mixed vegetable salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk with honey

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 to 2 liters of water daily
    Lemon water during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary sodas
    Processed snacks
    High-fat foods
