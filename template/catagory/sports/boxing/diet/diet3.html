    • BOXING DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,900 - 2,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 scrambled eggs
    1 slice of whole grain toast
    1 banana

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    1 orange

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (150g)
    1 cup of brown rice
    Steamed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 cup of Greek yogurt
    A handful of berries

    Dinner:

    Lean beef or fish (150g)
    Sweet potato
    Mixed green salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2 liters of water daily
    Herbal tea during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-fat snacks
    Sugary drinks
    Processed foods
