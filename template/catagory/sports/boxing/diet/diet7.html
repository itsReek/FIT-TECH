    • BOXING DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,000 - 2,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 egg whites and 1 whole egg
    1 slice of whole grain bread
    1 cup of mixed fruit

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Protein shake
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (200g)
    1 cup of brown rice
    Mixed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    Hummus with carrot and cucumber sticks

    Dinner:

    Baked salmon (150g)
    Quinoa
    Steamed broccoli

    Before Bed:

    1 small bowl of Greek yogurt

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Fried foods
    Sugary snacks
    High-calorie beverages
