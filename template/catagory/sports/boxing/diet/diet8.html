    • BOXING DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,200 - 2,400 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 egg whites and 1 whole egg
    1 whole wheat bagel
    1 glass of almond milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Protein bar
    1 apple

    Lunch:

    Turkey breast sandwich on whole grain bread
    Side salad with olive oil dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 cup of low-fat cottage cheese
    A handful of walnuts

    Dinner:

    Stir-fried tofu (200g)
    Brown rice
    Mixed vegetables

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 liters of water daily

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-sugar cereals
    Processed foods
    Sugary beverages
