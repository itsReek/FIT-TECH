FitTech

    • BOXING DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,000 - 2,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 egg whites and 1 whole egg omelet
    2 slices of whole grain bread
    1 bowl of oatmeal with fruit

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Protein smoothie with banana
    A handful of nuts

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (200g)
    1 cup of brown rice
    Mixed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    1 small container of Greek yogurt

    Dinner:

    Baked salmon or lean beef (200g)
    Sweet potato or quinoa
    Steamed broccoli

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk or protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    2 liters of water daily
    Coconut water during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-calorie snacks
    Fast food
    Sugary drinks
