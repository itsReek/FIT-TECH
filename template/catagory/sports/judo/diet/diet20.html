    • JUDO DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,800 - 3,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 scrambled eggs
    2 slices of whole grain toast
    1 cup of Greek yogurt with honey
    1 glass of smoothie with fruits

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A protein shake
    A handful of almonds

    Lunch:

    Grilled steak or tofu (250g)
    Brown rice or quinoa
    Large mixed salad

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 bowl of hummus with veggie sticks
    1 protein bar

    Dinner:

    Roast chicken or fish (250g)
    Couscous
    Grilled asparagus

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of casein protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    3 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drinks for recovery

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Processed sugary foods
    High-fat dairy products
    Excessive caffeine
