FitTech

    • JUDO DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,000 - 3,500 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 scrambled eggs
    2 slices of whole grain bread
    1 cup of oatmeal with fruits
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A protein bar
    A banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast (250g)
    Brown rice
    Large mixed vegetable salad

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 bowl of cottage cheese
    A handful of walnuts

    Dinner:

    Baked tilapia or cod (250g)
    Quinoa
    Steamed spinach

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of almond milk

    HYDRATION:

    3 to 4 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drinks after workouts

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-sugar foods
    Fried foods
    Heavy sauces
