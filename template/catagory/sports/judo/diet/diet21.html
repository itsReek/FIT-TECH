    • JUDO DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,600 - 3,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 scrambled eggs
    1 slice of whole grain toast
    1 banana
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 protein bar

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (200g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed broccoli

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    2 tablespoons of peanut butter

    Dinner:

    Fish (200g)
    Mashed sweet potatoes
    Mixed greens salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of low-fat yogurt

    HYDRATION:

    2 to 2.5 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drinks after workouts

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary drinks
    High-calorie snacks
    Excessive saturated fats
