    • JUDO DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,800 - 3,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 boiled eggs
    2 slices of whole grain toast
    1 cup of cottage cheese
    1 glass of juice

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    Protein shake
    A handful of almonds

    Lunch:

    Grilled turkey (200g)
    Sweet potato
    Large garden salad

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 serving of Greek yogurt
    1 cup of mixed berries

    Dinner:

    Baked salmon (250g)
    Quinoa
    Steamed vegetables

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of casein protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    3 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drinks during training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Processed snacks
    High-sugar cereals
    Excessive caffeine
