    • JUDO DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,500 - 4,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    4 whole eggs
    2 slices of whole grain toast
    1 avocado
    1 glass of smoothie

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 protein shake
    1 piece of fruit

    Lunch:

    Steak (250g)
    Baked potatoes
    Roasted vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 bowl of Greek yogurt
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Roast chicken (300g)
    Couscous
    Mixed greens salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of low-fat milk

    HYDRATION:

    3 to 4 liters of water daily
    Hydration drinks post-exercise

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Fast food
    Sugary snacks
    Excessive carbohydrates
