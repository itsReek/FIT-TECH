FitTech

    • JUDO DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,100 - 2,200 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 scrambled eggs
    1 slice of whole grain toast
    1 small bowl of muesli with bananas
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or paneer (150g)
    Brown rice or quinoa
    Steamed spinach

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 small yogurt
    A handful of sunflower seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled salmon or tofu (150g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Stir-fried mixed vegetables

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.2 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drink during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary drinks
    Fried foods
    Processed snacks
