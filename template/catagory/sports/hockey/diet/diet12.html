FitTech

    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,400 - 2,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 boiled egg with whole wheat toast
    1 bowl of oats with almond milk and fresh fruit
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or tofu (120g)
    Brown rice or whole wheat bread
    Steamed vegetables (spinach, carrots, broccoli)

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 orange
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (120g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil and lemon

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk or smoothie

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or sports drinks during/after intense training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed foods
    Deep-fried snacks
