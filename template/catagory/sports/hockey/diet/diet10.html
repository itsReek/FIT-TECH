FitTech

    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,900 - 2,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 scrambled egg
    1 slice of whole grain bread
    1 small bowl of fruit salad

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    1 glass of yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (100g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 banana
    A handful of pumpkin seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (100g)
    1 chapati
    Steamed vegetables (carrots, broccoli)

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk or herbal tea

    HYDRATION:

    2 to 2.5 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drinks after training sessions

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    High-fat snacks
    Sugary drinks
    Fried foods
