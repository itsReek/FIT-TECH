    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,400 - 3,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 boiled eggs with whole wheat toast
    1 bowl of oatmeal with chia seeds and fresh berries
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    1 apple

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or tofu (175g)
    Brown rice or quinoa
    Steamed vegetables (spinach, carrots, broccoli)

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 yogurt with mixed nuts
    1 orange

    Dinner:

    Grilled salmon or paneer (175g)
    Whole wheat chapati
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    3 liters of water daily
    Sports drinks during training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed foods
    Deep-fried snacks
