    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,700 - 1,800 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 boiled egg with avocado toast
    1 small bowl of oatmeal with fresh berries
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of cashews
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or paneer (100g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, spinach, carrots)

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 banana
    A handful of mixed seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (100g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Mixed vegetable salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk or a protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 to 2 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or electrolyte drinks after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Deep-fried snacks
    Processed food
