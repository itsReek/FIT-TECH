FitTech

    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,500 - 1,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 boiled egg with whole grain toast
    1 small bowl of oatmeal with fresh fruit
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of nuts (almonds or cashews)
    1 apple

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (100g)
    Brown rice or whole wheat bread
    Steamed broccoli and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 banana
    A handful of mixed seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (100g)
    1 whole wheat chapati
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 to 2 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drink during/after practice

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Deep-fried foods
    Processed food
