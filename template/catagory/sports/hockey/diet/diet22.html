FitTech

    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    3,200 - 3,400 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 whole eggs and 3 egg whites
    1 avocado on whole grain toast
    1 glass of fruit smoothie

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 cup of cottage cheese
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or chickpea salad
    1 cup of barley
    Steamed broccoli

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 protein shake
    1 banana

    Dinner:

    Baked fish or lentil stew
    1 cup of sweet potatoes
    Side salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of almond milk

    HYDRATION:

    3.5 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drinks during training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Processed snacks
    Sugary beverages
    High-fat dairy products
