    • HOCKEY DIET

    3,500 - 3,700 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 scrambled eggs with avocado
    1 bowl of oats with almond milk and berries
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of cashews
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (175g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Mixed vegetables

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 yogurt with honey
    1 apple

    Grilled fish or paneer (175g)
    Whole wheat chapati
    Salad with olive oil

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    3 liters of water daily
    Coconut water after training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed foods
    Fried snacks
