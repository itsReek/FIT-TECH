FitTech

    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,700 - 2,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 boiled eggs with whole wheat toast
    1 bowl of oats with almond milk and berries
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or tofu (150g)
    Brown rice or whole wheat pasta
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, spinach, and carrots)

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 orange
    A handful of mixed nuts

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (150g)
    Whole wheat chapati
    Mixed salad with olive oil and lemon

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Coconut water or sports drinks after intense sessions

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed foods
    Fried snacks
