FitTech

    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,400 - 2,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 scrambled eggs with spinach on whole wheat toast
    1 bowl of oatmeal with fresh fruit and chia seeds
    1 glass of milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds
    1 banana

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast or paneer (150g)
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Steamed broccoli, spinach, and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 yogurt with mixed seeds
    1 apple

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or tofu (150g)
    Whole wheat chapati
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil and lemon

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Electrolyte drinks or coconut water post-training

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks
    Processed snacks
    Deep-fried food
