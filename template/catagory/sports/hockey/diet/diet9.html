    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,800 - 1,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 boiled egg
    1 slice of whole grain bread
    1 small bowl of mixed fruits

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of peanuts
    1 glass of buttermilk

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or tofu (80g)
    Steamed rice or chapati
    Mixed vegetable curry

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 apple
    A handful of sunflower seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled fish or paneer (80g)
    1 chapati
    Salad (cucumber, tomato, onion)

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    1.5 to 2 liters of water daily
    Herbal tea after meals

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Fast food
    Sugary snacks
    Soft drinks
