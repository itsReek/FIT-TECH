    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,400 - 2,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 boiled eggs with avocado on toast
    1 bowl of oatmeal with nuts and honey
    1 glass of water with lemon

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    1 orange
    1 yogurt with chia seeds

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken (100g) or lentil soup
    Whole grain rice
    Steamed veggies

    Afternoon Snack:

    A handful of cashews
    1 cup of green tea

    Dinner:

    Baked fish or tofu stir fry
    Brown rice
    Mixed salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk

    HYDRATION:

    2.3 liters of water daily
    Sports drinks post-exercise

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Fried food
    Sugary beverages
    Processed snacks
