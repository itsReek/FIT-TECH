    • HOCKEY DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,000 - 2,100 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 omelet with vegetables
    1 slice of whole grain toast
    1 small bowl of mixed fruits

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of walnuts
    1 glass of yogurt

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or paneer (100g)
    Brown rice or whole wheat chapati
    Steamed broccoli and carrots

    Afternoon Snack:

    1 pear
    A handful of mixed seeds

    Dinner:

    Grilled salmon or tofu (100g)
    1 chapati
    Mixed vegetable salad

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm milk or a protein shake

    HYDRATION:

    2.5 to 3 liters of water daily
    Herbal teas after meals

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Processed snacks
    High-sugar foods
    Fried items
