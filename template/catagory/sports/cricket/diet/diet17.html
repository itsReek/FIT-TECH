    • CRICKET DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,200 to 2,600 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    2 boiled eggs
    1 bowl of oatmeal with honey and fruits
    1 glass of low-fat milk

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of almonds or walnuts
    A small fruit (banana or apple)

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or fish
    Brown rice or whole wheat chapati
    Mixed vegetables (spinach, carrots)
    1 bowl of dal

    Afternoon Snack:

    A protein bar or Greek yogurt
    A piece of fruit (orange or pear)

    Dinner:

    Vegetable soup or lentil soup
    Lean protein: Chicken or fish
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, peas)
    1 glass of buttermilk

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-8 glasses of water throughout the day.
    Include coconut water after training.

    Before Training:

    A small snack 1-2 hours before: Energy bar or banana.

    After Training:

    A post-workout meal: Protein shake with a piece of fruit within 30-60 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Sugary drinks (soda, energy drinks)
