FitTech

    • CRICKET DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,600 to 2,000 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 bowl of oatmeal with fruits and honey
    1 glass of milk or yogurt smoothie

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of nuts (almonds or walnuts)
    A small fruit (apple or banana)

    Lunch:

    A serving of grilled chicken or paneer
    Brown rice or whole wheat chapati
    Steamed mixed vegetables
    1 bowl of dal

    Afternoon Snack:

    A protein bar or a small serving of yogurt
    A piece of fruit (orange or banana)

    Dinner:

    Lentil soup
    Lean protein: Chicken or fish
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots)
    1 glass of buttermilk

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-8 glasses of water throughout the day.
    Include coconut water after training.

    BEFORE TRAINING:

    A small snack 1-2 hours before: A banana with peanut butter.

    AFTER TRAINING:

    A post-workout meal: Protein shake with a piece of fruit within 30-60 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Sugary drinks (soda, energy drinks)
