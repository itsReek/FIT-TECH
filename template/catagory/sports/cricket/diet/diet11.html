FitTech

    • CRICKET DIET

    CALORIES:

    1,800 to 2,300 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    1 bowl of oatmeal with banana and honey
    1 glass of low-fat milk or a protein shake

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts
    1 apple or orange

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken or paneer
    Quinoa or brown rice
    Mixed vegetable salad with olive oil dressing

    Afternoon Snack:

    Greek yogurt with honey and berries
    A piece of fruit (banana or pear)

    Dinner:

    Lentil soup
    Baked fish or chicken
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots)
    1 glass of buttermilk

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 6-8 glasses of water throughout the day.
    Include coconut water after training.

    BEFORE TRAINING:

    A small snack 1-2 hours before: Energy bar or banana.

    AFTER TRAINING:

    A post-workout meal: Protein shake with a piece of fruit within 30-60 minutes.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks (candy, cookies)
    Processed foods (chips, instant noodles)
    Fast food (burgers, fries)
    Sugary drinks (soda, energy drinks)
