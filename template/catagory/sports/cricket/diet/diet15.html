    • CRICKET DIET

    CALORIES:

    2,500 to 2,900 (depending on activity level)

    Breakfast:

    3 eggs (boiled, poached, or scrambled)
    2 slices of whole-grain toast with avocado or peanut butter
    1 medium-sized fruit (apple or banana)
    1 glass of low-fat milk or a protein smoothie

    Mid-Morning Snack:

    A handful of mixed nuts (almonds, walnuts, cashews)
    1 small Greek yogurt (plain, low-fat) with honey

    Lunch:

    Grilled chicken breast (200g) or lean fish (salmon, tuna)
    1.5 cups of brown rice or quinoa
    Steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, bell peppers)
    Mixed green salad with olive oil and balsamic vinegar

    Afternoon Snack:

    A smoothie made with spinach, banana, and a scoop of protein powder
    1 whole-grain granola bar

    Dinner:

    Lean beef (200g) or grilled turkey breast
    1 medium-sized baked sweet potato or whole-grain pasta
    Steamed or roasted vegetables (zucchini, cauliflower, asparagus)

    Before Bed:

    1 glass of warm low-fat milk
    A few slices of fresh fruit (pineapple, orange)

    HYDRATION:

    Drink 2.5 to 3 liters of water throughout the day.

    FOODS TO AVOID:

    Sugary snacks and drinks (sodas, candy)
    Fried or processed foods (chips, fast food)
    High-fat and high-sodium foods (processed meats)
